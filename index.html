Product Strategy

Zillow Rental Search — Product Teardown & RoomMatch Proposal

A self-initiated product teardown of Zillow's rental search feature — grounded in San Francisco's rental market. Maps the existing user flow, conducts a SWOT analysis, and identifies the critical gap in shared-living search. Proposes RoomMatch: a roommate-matching feature with a fully interactive 6-screen MVP prototype featuring dynamic compatibility scoring, real SF neighbourhoods and rent prices, BART and Caltrain commute tags, and a live split rent calculator.

"Zillow excels at helping users find where to live — but completely ignores who they will live with. RoomMatch closes that gap without users ever leaving the platform."

Urban Service Operations — SF 311 & the F1 Showrun

A personal project applying Difference-in-Differences regression to SF's open 311 dataset to measure the causal impact of the F1 Showrun on service complaints in the Marina neighborhood — controlling for citywide trends using all other SF neighborhoods as a control group.

"The F1 Showrun caused a statistically significant surge of +12.31 complaints per day in the Marina — an effect isolated to the neighborhood and not reflected in the rest of San Francisco."
